Here, All about Effort
The research conducted in this short study aims to examine and explore the importance of having an active online brand presence on Instagram. Do the brands which actively engage with consumers and appear to be readily and easily approachable have a higher degree of sales compared to brands which use this specific social platform to purely showcase their latest collection? And does the higher traffic that is generated from consumer brand engagement necessarily contribute to more sales.
